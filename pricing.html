Frequently Asked Questions

What is "Bring Your Own Key" (BYOK)?

With the free tier, you use your own OpenRouter API key. You pay OpenRouter directly for AI usage (typically $0.01-0.05 per image). This gives you unlimited access with no monthly fees.

What counts as a generation?

Each image created counts as 1 generation. If you generate 4 variations at once, that's 4 generations. Text-only outputs (like copywriting) are free and don't count.

Do unused generations roll over?

No, monthly generations reset each billing cycle. However, purchased credits never expire.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period, then revert to the free tier.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and some regional payment methods through Stripe.

Is there an Enterprise plan?

Yes! For unlimited generations, custom integrations, SLA, and dedicated support, contact us at enterprise@hefaistos.xyz
