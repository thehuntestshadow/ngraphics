Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NGRAPHICS.

NGRAPHICS is an AI-powered toolkit for creating professional e-commerce visuals. It includes 20+ specialized studios for generating product infographics, model photos, lifestyle shots, packaging mockups, social media graphics, and more.

Upload your product image, customize your settings, and let AI generate stunning marketing visuals in seconds.

Getting started is easy:

  • Go to any studio (like Infographics)
  • Enter your OpenRouter API key (free tier) or sign up for a Pro plan
  • Upload a product image
  • Customize your settings and click Generate

No account is required to use the free tier with your own API key.

With the free tier, you use your own OpenRouter API key. You pay OpenRouter directly for AI usage (typically $0.01-0.05 per image generation).

To get an API key:

  • Visit openrouter.ai
  • Create a free account
  • Go to Keys and create a new API key
  • Add credits to your OpenRouter account
  • Paste the key into NGRAPHICS

This gives you unlimited access to all studios with no monthly subscription.

NGRAPHICS includes 20+ specialized studios:

  • Visual: Infographics, Model Studio, Lifestyle, Bundle, Background, Packaging
  • Marketing: Social Studio, Ad Creative, Badges, Comparison, Feature Cards, A+ Content
  • Content: Copywriter, FAQ Generator, Size Chart, Size Visualizer
  • Utility: Product Variants, Export Center, Model Video
