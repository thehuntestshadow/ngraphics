Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

HEFAISTOS ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered e-commerce toolkit at hefaistos.xyz.

02

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted and hashed)
  • Display name (optional)

Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you use our service:

  • Generation history (images created, prompts used)
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Session duration and frequency
  • Device type and browser information

Uploaded Content

When you use our tools, you may upload:

  • Product images for AI processing
  • Reference images for style matching
  • Brand logos and assets

Payment Information

Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store your full credit card details. We receive only:

  • Last 4 digits of your card
  • Card brand (Visa, Mastercard, etc.)
  • Billing address
  • Transaction history
03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Process your AI image generations
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Send important service notifications
  • Improve our tools and user experience
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations
04

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate HEFAISTOS:

Supabase (Authentication & Database)

We use Supabase for user authentication, database storage, and file storage. Supabase processes your account data and uploaded images.

Supabase Privacy Policy

Stripe (Payments)

We use Stripe to process subscription payments. Stripe handles all payment card data securely.

Stripe Privacy Policy

OpenRouter (AI Processing)

We use OpenRouter to access AI models for image generation. Your uploaded images and prompts are sent to OpenRouter for processing.

OpenRouter Privacy Policy

AI Model Training

Your uploaded images are NOT used to train AI models. They are only used for your specific generation requests and are not retained by AI providers beyond the processing session.

05

Data Storage & Security

Where We Store Your Data

  • Account data: Supabase servers (AWS infrastructure)
  • Generated images: Supabase Storage with encrypted buckets
  • Local cache: Your browser's IndexedDB for offline access

Security Measures

  • All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt
  • Row-level security (RLS) on all database tables
  • Regular security audits

Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Generation history: Retained for 12 months, then auto-archived
  • Payment records: Retained as required by law (typically 7 years)

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, go to Settings > Data & Privacy, or contact us at privacy@hefaistos.xyz.

07

Cookies & Local Storage

We use the following client-side storage:

Essential (Required)

  • Authentication tokens: Keep you logged in
  • Session data: Maintain your current session

Functional (Preferences)

  • Theme preference: Light/dark mode setting
  • Language preference: UI and generation language
  • UI settings: Collapsed sections, view preferences

Performance (Cache)

  • IndexedDB: Cache generated images for faster loading
  • Service Worker: Cache static assets for offline use

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies.
